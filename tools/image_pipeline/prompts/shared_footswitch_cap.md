# Shared Footswitch Cap — Default Pack

**Status:** v1 — untested.
**Audience:** every default-pack pedal uses this one footswitch cap image.
**Output target:** transparent PNG, top-down, 512 × 512.

## What we're generating

The **cap** that protrudes through the centre hole of the pedal cover — the
small chrome dome the cover "presses." JUCE composites it at the cover's
centre and animates it downward with the cover on press.

## Prompt

```
Photorealistic top-down product photograph of a single 3PDT footswitch
cap — the small metal button that protrudes through the centre hole of
a Boss-style guitar pedal cover. Viewed from directly above, perfectly
orthographic, NOT perspective, NOT angled, NOT 3/4.

The cap is a small POLISHED CHROME / MIRROR-FINISH stainless steel dome.
Round, slightly domed convex top (gently curved upward at the centre).
About as wide as it is round (perfect circle in plan view).

A soft specular highlight near the upper portion of the dome implying
overhead studio lighting. A subtle reflection of the studio light should
make the metal look real and convex — but the dome should still read as
unified metal, not over-blown white.

ONLY the cap is visible — no threaded shaft underneath, no nut, no
mounting hardware, no lug terminals, no wires. These would be inside the
enclosure in real hardware; we want only what the user would see from
above.

Pure white background. Isolated subject — just the cap, no chassis, no
cover, no other components, no shadows on the background.

Sharp focus. Professional product photography. Photorealistic. Square
1:1 crop, subject centred with whitespace around for bounding-box
detection.
```

## Negative prompts

```
perspective, 3/4, angled, tilted, side view, exposed shaft, threaded post,
hex nut, mounting nut, lock washer, solder lugs, lug terminals, wires,
wire leads, exposed wiring, multiple switches, switch in cover, mounted,
illustration, cartoon, sketch, 3D render obvious CGI, painted, colored,
gradient color, multiple objects, hands, finger, foot, instrument
```

## Acceptance bar

Keep:
- Pure top-down circular cap
- Polished chrome / mirror finish
- Just the dome visible, no shaft / nut / lugs underneath
- White background, isolated

Reject:
- Side or angled view
- Visible threaded shaft, nut, wires, lugs
- Painted / colored switch
- Multiple switches
- Switch shown mounted in a cover
